Overview

WASH NOW is a mobile application concept designed to simplify the laundry service experience for busy urban dwellers. Developed as a key project for my User Experience Design course, the app bridges the gap between laundry service providers and customers through a seamless digital platform.

The project focused on addressing common pain points such as tracking order status, finding nearby laundry services, and transparent pricing. Through iterative design and user testing, WASH NOW delivers a solution that is both intuitive and efficient.

Design Goals

Real-time Tracking

Enable users to monitor their laundry status from pickup to delivery in real-time.

Location Services

Help users find and select the nearest or best-rated laundry services in their vicinity.

Trust & Transparency

Provide transparent pricing and clear service options to build user trust.

Key Features

01

Service Selection

Easy-to-use interface for selecting different types of laundry services (Wash & Fold, Dry Cleaning, etc.).

02

Live Tracking

Visual timeline showing exactly what stage the laundry is in (Washing, Drying, Folding, Ready).

03

Pickup & Delivery

Integrated scheduling for convenient pickup and delivery times to suit user schedules.

04

Secure Payments

Multiple digital payment options for a cashless and hassle-free transaction.
