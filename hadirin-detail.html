Overview

Hadirin is a comprehensive employee attendance and wellbeing application designed for the retail industry as part of a UI/UX Design competition. The project addresses critical challenges in retail operations including unfair shift distribution and employee wellbeing management.

Through extensive user research, brainstorming sessions, and a human-centered design approach, Hadirin provides an innovative solution that balances operational efficiency with employee satisfaction. The design process utilized Figma for UI design and FigJam for collaborative ideation and user journey mapping.

Problem Statement

Unfair Shift Distribution

Retail employees often face inconsistent and unfair scheduling leading to burnout and dissatisfaction

Employee Wellbeing

Limited focus on employee mental health and work-life balance in traditional systems

Manual Processes

Time-consuming manual attendance tracking and shift management causing inefficiencies

Key Features

01

Smart Attendance System

Easy clock-in/out with GPS verification and real-time attendance tracking for accurate records

02

Fair Shift Scheduling

AI-powered shift distribution algorithm ensuring equitable workload among employees

03

Wellbeing Dashboard

Track employee wellness metrics, request time off, and access mental health resources

04

User Research Driven

Designed based on comprehensive user interviews and brainstorming with retail workers

Design Process

User Research

Collaborative brainstorming sessions to generate creative solutions

Prototyping in Figma

High-fidelity prototypes with user testing and iterative refinements
